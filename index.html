Your Marriage Connection Profile: The Journey Begins

Welcome to your personalized guide to understanding each other in a new way. This profile is built on the groundbreaking research of Dr. Scott Inman, PhD, and his Covenant Intimacy Fulfillment Model™ (CIFM). It's designed to be a living map to the unique landscape of your relationship, not a set of labels. It will help you name what's true today and aim for what's possible tomorrow.

Your results are organized into six sections:

  • The CIFM Overview (the big picture)
  • Your Intimacy Preferences (how you connect)
  • Relational Dynamics (how you interact)
  • Connection & Conflict Insights (how you navigate challenges)
  • A Shared Action Plan (what to do next)
  • A Partner Alignment Worksheet (for side-by-side comparison)

Each section begins with a brief explanation to help you understand what you're seeing and how to use it. At the end of each section, you will find Discussion Prompts to help you turn these insights into action. We encourage you to use your strengths intentionally and explore one new practice together this week.

The Covenant Intimacy Fulfillment Model™ (CIFM) Map

The CIFM is a powerful map that gives you a shared language to navigate your relationship. It organizes intimacy into 12 types across four key categories—balancing the essential aspects of “being together” and “doing together”. As you explore your profile, you'll see how these types create a unique picture of your connection.

  • Core: Spiritual, Emotional, Commitment
  • Relational: Intellectual, Affable, Recreational
  • Partnership: Partnership, Stability, Creative, Conflict
  • Physical: Physical, Sexual
